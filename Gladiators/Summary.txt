You are a gladiator and your goal is to beat the arena boss.

How to play:
You start with 100 ATK, 100 HP, 100 money. With money you can buy items that will 
boost your ATK and HP. If an item is bought, you can't buy the previous weaker versions
of the item. You can get money by defeating the monsters in the forest.

Fighting: turn based 
The green sword does the least damage but has a 100% chance to hit.
The yellow sword does more damage but has a 80% chance to hit.
The red sword does the most damage but has a 50% chance to hit.

Note: The game is saved and you can continue after you quit.
